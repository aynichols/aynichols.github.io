Completely surrendered into the hands of God

January 15, 2017

This has been a very crazy time. We have had up and downs but every day I have been filled by the peace of God. I have given Him my complete trust and hope. He knows my hearth and I know He is walking with me through whatever is coming now. Battling my toughest cancer but I have already passed most odds that I have had against me.

Now, one day at the time fighting hard and making the best of everything.
